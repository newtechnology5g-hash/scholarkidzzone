🏫 About Us

Nurturing Young Minds

Building the foundation for a lifetime of learning

Welcome to Scholar Kidz Zone Pre Primary School — a place where every child's journey of learning begins with joy, wonder, and play. Located in the heart of Ujwal Nagar, Belgaum, Karnataka, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 3 to 8 years.

Our experienced and caring teachers use play-based methodologies to develop cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills in every child. We believe every child is unique and deserves a personalised approach to early education.
